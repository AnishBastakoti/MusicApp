FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.

  • What is Musicapp?
    Musicapp is a streaming service that allows you to access millions of songs, curated playlists, and exclusive content.
  • How much does MusicApp cost?
    MusicApp offers different subscription plans. The individual plan costs $9.99 per month, while the family plan costs $14.99 per month and allows up to six people to share the subscription.
  • Can I listen to MusicApp offline?
    Yes, with MusicApp, you can download songs, albums, and playlists to your device for offline listening. This feature is available for both iOS and Android devices.
  • Can I access MusicApp on multiple devices?
    Absolutely! You can access MusicApp on multiple devices, including iPhone, iPad, Mac, PC, Apple Watch, and Android devices. Your library and preferences will sync across all your devices.
  • Is there a free trial available for MusicApp?
    Yes, MusicApp offers a free trial period of three months for new subscribers. During this trial period, you can enjoy all the features of the service without any charges.
